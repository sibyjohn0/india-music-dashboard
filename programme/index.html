For independent artists · 6 months

Make the music.
Run the business.

Most independent artists in India are making the music and guessing the rest. No one around who's done it, no framework for the decisions that actually determine whether it goes anywhere — distribution, deal structure, promotion budgets, how to build a fan base that pays. They figure it out alone, mid-career, by making expensive mistakes.

We work with a small group of artists over six months to close that gap. Not by making decisions for them — by teaching them to make the right ones themselves. Flat fee, no rights, no commission. Everything they build, they keep.

We also map what you already have. Most artists underestimate their existing network — producers, collaborators, venues, people who already believe in them. We treat those relationships as leverage from the start and help you think about them long-term and on your own terms.

Does this sound like your year?

The track that never came out
A song you finished two months ago. Still unreleased because one thing is missing. The moment passes. The track goes cold.
The plan that died in February
A content strategy that made sense in January. Fourteen posts in, it stopped. Nobody asked why. Nobody noticed.
The list that never moves
Forty tasks. No sense of which three matter this week. Every month the same items sit at the top.
The spend with no return
Money on a mix, a shoot, a campaign. No measure of whether it worked. No plan for what happens after it drops.
The isolation of building alone
Decisions made in a vacuum. No one around who has done it before. No peers navigating the same market, the same constraints, the same questions.

If two of these are true, this programme is worth a conversation.
