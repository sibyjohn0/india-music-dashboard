The programme

Six phases. One focus per month.

The first two months are team-led. From month three onward, you execute and we review weekly. The task list we build together in month two is the anchor for every session through month six.

1
Month
Financial Diagnostic
Team leads
  • Income and cost audit: last six months on the table
  • Monthly income target set, tracked from here
  • Singles vs EP direction decided
  • Deal structure education: what you are signing and what it costs you
  • Problems named, not softened
Output: Financial snapshot + monthly target
2
Month
Identity and Production
Team leads
  • Music audit: what is working, what is not, what to do next
  • Identity work: the one thing you will be known for
  • EPK built from scratch or rebuilt if it exists
  • Social media direction and content plan
  • Designers, photographers, VO artists briefed and moving
  • Task list built together: the spine of months three through six
Output: EPK + content plan + task list
3
Month
Distribution and Outreach
Artist executes
  • Streaming profiles audited and fixed
  • Playlist outreach: first pitches sent using the EPK
  • Cold outreach to blogs, press, and music media
  • Weekly check-in against task list begins
Output: First 10 pitches sent and tracked
4
Month
Live Circuit
Artist executes
  • Three self-booked shows: venue cold outreach using the EPK
  • One booking agency approached with a proper pitch
  • Show documentation: attendance, setlist, video, contacts made
Output: 3 shows confirmed or in pipeline
5
Month
Community
Artist executes
  • WhatsApp fan group started and seeded
  • 50 named fans identified: real people, direct relationships
  • Direct engagement: five conversations per week minimum
  • Fan-to-revenue pathway sketched
Output: 50 named fans, active fan group
6
Month
Income Activation
Together
  • One non-streaming income stream activated: sync, merch, licensing, direct-to-fan
  • Six-month review: what moved, what did not, what was learned
  • Next six months planned — by you, with our input
Output: One income stream live + 6-month review doc
The weekly rhythm
Line items. Every week. From month two onward.

The task list built in session two is the anchor for every check-in. We go through it together: what shipped, what is blocked, what the one thing to fix is this week. If it is not on the list, it is not this week's conversation — it gets added and reviewed next session.

WhatsApp is async. You send questions any time. We respond in our own time. The weekly session is where things get resolved, not every message thread.

"We move at the speed of the task list, not the speed of the anxiety."
