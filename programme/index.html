The consultants

We start with what you already have. Every artist comes in with something real: a sound, a partial network, skills built on your own, momentum you may not see clearly yet. The programme

Six phases. One focus per month.

You own your workstream from day one. The consultant sessions are where we bring the thinking: diagnosis, direction, challenge. Between sessions, you execute. We review output, not plans.

The weekly rhythm
We review output. Not plans.

The task list from month two is the anchor. Every week you show us what shipped. If something did not ship, we ask why — and the item either resets with a new deadline or goes to a specialist at your cost. We do not do it for you. We do not let it slide.

WhatsApp is async. You send questions any time. We respond in our own time. The weekly session is where things get resolved — not every message thread. If it is not on the task list, it is not this week's conversation.

"We move at the speed of the task list, not the speed of the anxiety."
The rule
Do it yourself or pay someone to do it.

If you own a task and you do not complete it by the agreed date, it goes to a specialist and you pay for it. Not a fine — just the market rate for getting someone else to do what you said you would do. We agreed the production budget in month one. The cost was always there. We just made it real.

Doing it yourself is always the right first answer. Only commission a specialist when the task genuinely needs one. Pay only for what is essential.

"Do it yourself or pay someone. Doing nothing is not an option."
