Artist Development Programme

You're already moving.
You just can't see what's in the way.

You're releasing music, playing shows, building something real, and the next level still feels further off than the work you're putting in should make it. You think it's the music. It usually isn't. It's everything around the music: the economics, the identity, the network, the way you release. That's the part no one taught you, and it's the part we work on.

Does this sound like your year?

The track that never came out
A song you finished two months ago. Still unreleased because one thing is missing. The moment passes. The track goes cold.
The plan that died in February
A content strategy that made sense in January. Fourteen posts in, it stopped. Nobody asked why. Nobody noticed.
The list that never moves
Forty tasks. No sense of which three matter this week. Every month the same items sit at the top.
The spend with no return
Money on a mix, a shoot, a campaign. No measure of whether it worked. No plan for what happens after it drops.
The isolation of building alone
Decisions made in a vacuum. No one around who has done it before. No peers navigating the same market, the same constraints, the same questions.

If two of these are true, this programme is worth a conversation.

What the first month looks like

Start with one month. ₹10,000. No commitment beyond that.

In the first two weeks, you share three things: your expenses for the last six months, your phone book (who you know), and your ambitions (who you want to be in eighteen months).

In the next two weeks, we map what we see: the gaps, the leverage you're already sitting on, and the specific things blocking the next rung.

At the end of the month, you get a document: your twelve-month picture, your KPIs, and exactly what stands between you and the next level, named specifically. That document is yours to keep, whatever you decide next.

If you want to keep going

If the first month is useful and you want to continue, month two onwards is a structured working relationship at the same ₹10,000 a month. Now you have the map, and we execute against it together. Continuing is your choice, not a default.
