India Indie Music Radar

Submit your music

Add yourself to the radar. We track Indian independent artists across YouTube, Spotify, and SoundCloud. No editorial gatekeeping. If you're making independent music, you belong here.

What happens after you submit New submissions are reviewed weekly. If your YouTube channel or Spotify profile has public listener data, you'll be added to the next data pull. You'll appear in the Discover tab, Artists tab, and Trends. There's no minimum follower count — we track emerging artists from the start.
