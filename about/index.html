Why we built this
The system was never designed for most artists.
Siby John & Karishma Changroth

We built this from the two sides of the gap most independent artists fall into: the business they were never taught, and the identity they have not yet crystallised. The tools here are free and always will be.

Siby spent thirteen years inside the platforms that decide which music gets seen. As Marketing Manager at YouTube India from 2022 to 2025 he ran creator and music programmes with 400+ artists and labels, including Anuv Jain, Jasleen Royal, and Jassie Gill, and is now Senior Creator Manager at LinkedIn. He saw how the system really surfaces music versus how artists assume it works.

Karishma is a Founding Member at Talented, Bangalore's independent creative agency. She brings brand identity, social strategy, and positioning: how an artist is perceived, packaged, and remembered. Between them, the two sides cover what blocks most independent artists.
